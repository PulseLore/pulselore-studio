Privacy

Privacy-first studio tools.

PulseLore Studio is being built as a public website and browser-based music workflow ecosystem. Phase 1 tools are designed to keep sensitive work local wherever possible.

Browser-local tools

Music Meter V1 analyzes selected audio locally in the browser. Talk With Zen Phase 1 and Creation Log drafts use local browser storage unless a future backend is explicitly connected.

No training claim

PulseLore Studio does not use uploaded or selected audio from the current static pages to train AI models. Phase 1 does not include server upload storage for these tools.

Local storage

Some pages may save drafts, logs, or preferences in your browser. You can clear them from the page controls or by clearing browser site data.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to contact@pulselore.studio.
