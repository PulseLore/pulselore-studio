Static-site publishing workflow

This version works now without a backend. Requests arrive by email; Zen manages them here locally; only safe public fields are exported to the website.

1 · Receive

Customer submits the 9-step request and sends the prepared email to PulseLore.

2 · Import

Copy the email body, click “Paste Request Email,” and create the customer record.

3 · Manage

Move the customer through Contacted, Price Confirmed, Building, Review, Approved, and Published.

4 · Publish

Check public approval, export portfolios.json, replace the repo file, Commit, and Push.
