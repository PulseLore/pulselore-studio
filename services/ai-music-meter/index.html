Beta preview limitations

Beta preview - real upload and backend analysis are not enabled yet. Selected audio is analyzed locally in your browser where possible. Results are risk estimates, not a guaranteed AI detector, not platform approval, and not copyright safety certification.

Real browser metrics

File name, size, duration, sample rate, channel count, peak, RMS, clipping risk, silence, dynamic range, and stereo balance where available.

Prototype estimates

AI-like, human-like, hybrid, artifact, vocal, pronunciation, distribution, and rights notes remain manual prototype risk estimates.

Future backend

Later flow: Google login, Supabase, temporary storage, Python analyzer, ffmpeg processing, and 24-hour auto-delete cron job.

Release guidance, not a guarantee

PulseLore AI Music Meter is a distribution preparation assistant preview. It highlights issues to review before release, but final legal clearance, platform decisions, and distributor approvals remain outside this beta tool.
